
# RIASEC Personality Model

Realistic: Prefers physical activities, tools, and machinery. Practical and hands-on.
Investigative: Analytical, intellectual, enjoys research and solving problems.
Artistic: Creative, expressive, enjoys art, design, music.
Social: Helpful, friendly, enjoys teaching or healing others.
Enterprising: Persuasive, leadership-oriented, enjoys starting projects or selling ideas.
Conventional: Organized, detail-oriented, enjoys structured tasks and working with data.
